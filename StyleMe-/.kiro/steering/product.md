# Product overview — StyleMe (web)

## Purpose

Help people explore **hairstyle ideas** that fit their **face shape** and **occasion** (work, events, casual refresh), with a clear, guided flow from photo upload through suggestions to saved results.

## Target users

- People unsure which cuts or styles suit their face
- Users planning for a specific occasion
- Anyone who wants quick, visual-style guidance before visiting a stylist

## Key product goals (web)

- **Low friction:** sign-in optional; fast path from landing to upload and ideas
- **Trust and clarity:** explain what the app does with face/photo data; obvious demo vs. “real” analysis if models are mocked
- **Shareable outcomes:** readable result cards; optional account / cloud save when backend exists
- **Accessible UI:** keyboard-friendly flows, readable type, sufficient contrast (see UI steering if added later)

## Out of scope (unless explicitly added)

- Replacing professional stylists or medical advice
- Storing raw photos without user consent and clear policy

## Success criteria

Users can complete: **upload → face / style step → personalized suggestions → optional save** without installing a mobile app.
